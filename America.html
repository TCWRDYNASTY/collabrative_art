Raíces de América

Digital Media

This piece is a call to return to the melting pot that made us great, honoring the promise to welcome all who yearn to breathe free. It challenges us to look past modern divisions and choose to listen, accept, and love one another regardless of race, ethnicity, sexual orientation, political party, or age. By trading blame for a human embrace, we prove that the only thing stronger than hate is love.
